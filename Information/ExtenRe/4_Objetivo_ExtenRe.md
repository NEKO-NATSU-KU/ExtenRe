## 🎯 What is the goal of ExtenRe?

The main objective of **ExtenRe** is to **revive and maintain open source projects that have been abandoned or suspended**, as well as those at risk of becoming obsolete due to lack of updates.

### Specific goals:

1. **Rescue valuable code**: Many abandoned projects contain useful ideas and functionalities that deserve to continue existing.
2. **Technological updating**: Migrate to recent versions of Java, Gradle, APIs, and other tools to ensure compatibility and security.
3. **Facilitate continuity**: Allow other developers to start from an updated base to continue adding functionalities.
4. **Share knowledge**: Document the changes made to serve as a reference for future reconstructions.

ExtenRe does not seek to replace the original projects, but to **extend their useful life** through careful and respectful reconstruction of the previous work.
