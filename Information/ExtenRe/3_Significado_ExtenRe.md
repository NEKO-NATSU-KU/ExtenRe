## 🔤 What does ExtenRe mean?

The name **ExtenRe** comes from the combination of two ideas:

- **Exten**: Short for "Extension"
- **Re**: Short for "Reconstructed", "Rebuilt", or "Restructured"

Therefore, **ExtenRe means "Reconstructed Extension"**.

This name perfectly reflects the nature of the project: taking an existing extension or project that has become obsolete or abandoned, and **rebuilding it** to give it new life, technologically updating it and ensuring its functionality in the present.
